About TAC

Professional Team Photo Placeholder

Lake Country's Premier Eco-Friendly Junk Removal Experts

Since 2015, TAC Junk Removal has been the trusted choice for environmentally responsible junk removal throughout Oconomowoc, Lake Country, and Waukesha County. Our professional team combines industry expertise with sustainable practices to deliver superior service.

We specialize in complete property cleanouts, commercial waste management, construction debris removal, and eco-friendly disposal solutions. Our commitment to environmental stewardship sets us apart—we divert 85% of collected materials from landfills through strategic recycling and donation partnerships.

Choose TAC for transparent pricing, professional service, and sustainable solutions that protect our Lake Country environment for future generations.
