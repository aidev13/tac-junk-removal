Lake Country's Trusted Junk Removal Experts Since 2015

With over 8 years of experience serving Oconomowoc, Lake Country, and the greater Waukesha County area, TAC Junk Removal has helped thousands of homeowners and businesses reclaim their space. We're fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your peace of mind.

Our professional team specializes in same-day junk removal, furniture pickup, appliance disposal, construction debris cleanup, and complete property cleanouts throughout Hartland, Delafield, Pewaukee, Nashotah, and surrounding Lake Country communities. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

We're committed to environmental responsibility - we donate, recycle, or properly dispose of 80% of items we collect, keeping them out of landfills whenever possible. Choose TAC for reliable service you can trust throughout Lake Country Wisconsin.
